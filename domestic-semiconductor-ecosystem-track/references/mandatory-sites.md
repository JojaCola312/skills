# Mandatory Sites

Check every site in this list on every invocation of the skill, even if broad search results already look complete.

The list supports national semiconductor ecosystem tracking first. Regional sources can still be retained, but they should not define the default national scope.

## A. National Core Official Sources

### 工业和信息化部
- Focus: 集成电路、半导体、重点项目、产业政策、制造业专项
- URLs:
  - https://www.miit.gov.cn/
- Why forced: Top national source for semiconductor industry policy, major projects, and official industrial signals.

### 科学技术部
- Focus: 科技计划、重点专项、实验室、科技奖励、科研平台
- URLs:
  - https://www.most.gov.cn/
- Why forced: Core national source for project, platform, and award signals.

### 国家自然科学基金委员会
- Focus: 半导体相关科研项目、资助方向、重点项目
- URLs:
  - https://www.nsfc.gov.cn/
- Why forced: Stable national source for research-project and discipline-level funding signals.

### 国家科学技术奖励工作办公室
- Focus: 国家科技奖、提名、公示、获奖结果
- URLs:
  - https://www.nosta.gov.cn/
- Why forced: Core national source for official award signals.

### 人力资源和社会保障部
- Focus: 国家级人才、职称、专业技术人才、表彰奖励
- URLs:
  - https://www.mohrss.gov.cn/
- Why forced: National source for talent-plan, title, and recognition signals.

## D. Maintenance Rules

- Prefer the newest official notice or list page when one exists.
- Keep stable top-level official portals even when article URLs rotate.
- Add dedicated semiconductor park, lab, or award pages when they become recurring signal sources.
- Keep national ministry and national award sources in the mandatory list even when a run is regionally focused.
- Treat local sources as regional supplements rather than the default national boundary.
- When adding a new required site, record:
  - region or institution name
  - tracking focus
  - official URL first
  - why it is mandatory
