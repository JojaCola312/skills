# Mandatory Conference Sites

Check every site in this list on every invocation of the skill, even if company websites already provide some updates.

The purpose is to catch competitor participation that may appear on conference sites before it appears on company sites.

## International Core Sites

### WiPDA

- URLs:
  - https://www.wipda.org/
  - https://www.psma.com/conferences/WiPDA
- Why forced: Dedicated wide-bandgap power-device meeting with sponsor and speaker visibility.

### ISPSD

- URLs:
  - https://www.ispsd2025.com/
  - https://www.ispsd2024.com/
- Why forced: Major power semiconductor event with exhibitor, sponsor, and technical program signals.

### ICSCRM

- URLs:
  - https://icscrm2025.org/
  - https://www.iisb.fraunhofer.de/en/events/2025/2025-09-14-to-19-icscrm-2025.html
- Why forced: Core global SiC meeting where ecosystem and company participation is highly visible.

## Domestic China Core Sites

### 慕尼黑上海电子展 / PCIM Asia 等功率电子相关展会官方站点

- URLs:
  - https://www.electronicachina.com.cn/
  - https://pcimasia-expo.cn/
- Why forced: Competitors often disclose booth, product, and partnership signals through these events.

## Maintenance Rules

- Prefer the current edition page if available.
- Keep at least one stable organizer page when event microsites rotate yearly.
- Add exhibitor or sponsor subpages if they are especially informative.
