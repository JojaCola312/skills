# Mandatory Sites

Check every site in this list on every invocation of the skill, even if broad search results already look complete.

## International Core Sites

### WiPDA

- Name: IEEE Workshop on Wide Bandgap Power Devices and Applications
- Type: Official conference / organizer page
- URLs:
  - https://www.wipda.org/
  - https://www.psma.com/conferences/WiPDA
- Why forced: One of the clearest dedicated WBG power-device meetings.

### ICSCRM

- Name: International Conference on Silicon Carbide and Related Materials
- Type: Official conference site / official host page
- URLs:
  - https://icscrm2025.org/
  - https://www.iisb.fraunhofer.de/en/events/2025/2025-09-14-to-19-icscrm-2025.html
- Why forced: Core global SiC conference.

## Maintenance Rules

- Prefer the newest current-edition page when one exists.
- Keep old edition sites if they are part of a rotating conference family and still point to the next host.
- Replace any dead link only after finding a higher-confidence official source.
- When adding a new required site, record:
  - conference name
  - why it is mandatory
  - official URL first
