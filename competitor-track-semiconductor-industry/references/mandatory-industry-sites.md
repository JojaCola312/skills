# Mandatory Industry Source Families

Use this file for broad industry-source discovery on every invocation.

Role of these sites:

- discover competitor signals that may not yet appear on company websites
- find clues for new product pages, partner announcements, capacity news, and customer progress
- provide lead-generation inputs for follow-up verification

Do not treat these sites as equal to official company confirmation.

Use them to discover leads first, then follow through to company, government, exchange, park, or partner detail pages.

## A-tier Discovery Sources

These are relatively focused on semiconductor or power-device industry updates and are worth checking every run.

### Future Semiconductor / 未来半导体

- URLs:
  - https://www.fsemi.tech/cms/semiconductor.html
  - https://www.fsemi.tech/cms/xinwenkuaixun.html
- Why included:
  - relatively concentrated semiconductor industry coverage
  - often surfaces product, partnership, and expansion signals early

### 行家说三代半

- URLs:
  - https://www.hjssemi.com/ev/list
- Why included:
  - useful for domestic third-generation semiconductor and power-device ecosystem developments
  - often exposes company-side activity and product updates before wider pickup

### 芯榜 / ICSMART

- URLs:
  - https://www.icsmart.cn/
- Why included:
  - focused semiconductor industry information source with practical competitor clues

### 电子元件技术网 / ECMR

- URLs:
  - http://ecmr.org.cn/Article/InfoList
- Why included:
  - useful for power electronics, devices, and component-side competitor developments

## B-tier Supplement Sources

These may still be useful, but they are broader or less semiconductor-focused. Use them as supplements rather than core discovery sources.

### EET-China

- URLs:

- Why included:

  - can surface signals, but should not dominate discovery logic

## Multi-Hop Rule

For every industry source used:

1. open the entry page
2. open the relevant article or listing page
3. continue to a company page, official product page, government page, partner page, or other stronger source when available

If the review stops at the media listing page, it is incomplete.

## Usage Rules

- use A-tier every run
- use B-tier when A-tier and company sources still leave gaps
- do not use media pages as the sole basis for major claims when stronger sources are available
- keep the official company link first in final output whenever a stronger source is found
